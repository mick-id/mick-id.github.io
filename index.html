Working with Fracture ID

We employ a world-class team of engineers, geologists, and geophysicists to integrate high-resolution Drillbit Geomechanics™, providing our clients with a cost-effective way to optimize their wells. We strive to meet each client’s unique challenges in the unconventional world. You can expect personal interaction and guidance from our team of industry experts from pre-spud all the way through to production. Reducing drilling and completion costs without affecting production is our priority. But that’s not all we do.

Reservoir Characterization

In today’s market, most companies rely solely on gamma-ray technology to characterize the formation and design a completion. Fracture ID’s Drillbit Geomechanics™ uses triaxial, high-frequency accelerometer data acquired during drilling operations to determine geomechanical properties along the wellbore. Drillbit Geomechanics™ is the foundation for data-driven workflows, giving our clients the tools they need to extract more from their wells.

Completion Application

Fracture ID’s team of completion experts is ready to assist operators’ engineering groups in leveraging the inherent heterogeneity along lateral wells, saving costs without sacrificing production. We can analyze your current completion strategy’s expected performance by conducting a fully integrated optimization using Drillbit Geomechanics™

COMPLETIONS ID

Drilling Application

Fracture ID combines information from high-resolution accelerometer data, geomechanical data, and traditional drilling data from the rig to determine the optimal drilling horizon in the target zone. Fracture ID’s drilling analysis uses downhole data to take a deeper look into drilling performance, drilling dysfunction, and off-bottom practices to drive optimization beyond what is possible with surface data alone.
